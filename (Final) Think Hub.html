Scientific Calculator with basic mathematical functions such as addition, subtraction, multiplication, and division. Also has more advanced functions such as trigometric ratios, (sine, cosine, tangent) square and cube roots, etc.







Online dictionary, provides the definition of word, part of speech as well as the origin of the word. Provides syllable and gives several examples of how to use the word as well as a feature that pronounces the word for you. The online dictionary also has a British dictionary, showing how the word would be used in the United Kingdom.







This link is to an online thesaurus. Similar to the online dictionary this site allows you to type in a word to get synonyms or antonyms of it. Once you have searched a word you can specify your results to have a word that is a certain length.




This link is to an online atlas. This contains information pertaining to different geographical features of the world such as rivers, lakes, oceans, and latitude and longitude positions. Also has information political features such as the flags of different countries, population, and cities.




This link is to an online encyclopedia. Similar to the online dictionary this site allows you to type in a word to get a variety of information on it. Unlike the thesaurus and dictionary the encyclopedia can be used to research topics. The site has a list of popular topics at the bottom as well as a search bar.







This link is to a gmail. This link was included so you can quickly communicate results with group members (if you were doing a project).
